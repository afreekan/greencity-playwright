**Description:** Verify that the user can select between 1 and 3 tags from the predefined list.

**Preconditions:**

- The user is logged into the system.
- The "Create News" form is open.

**Steps:**

1. Navigate to [GreenCity News](https://www.greencity.cx.ua/#/greenCity/news) and click "Create News".
2. In the "Tag" field, select one tag ("News").
3. Fill in the required fields:

- Title: "Test"
- Main Text: "Test content with 20 chars"

4. Click "Publish".
5. Verify that the news is published with the "News" tag.
6. Open the "Create News" form again.
7. Select three tags: "News", "Events", "Education".
8. Click "Publish".
9. Verify that the news is published with all three selected tags.
10. Attempt to select a fourth tag ("Initiatives").
11. Verify that selecting a fourth tag is blocked.

**Expected Result:**

- The user must select at least one and at most three tags.
- Selecting more than three tags is not possible.

**Automated Verification:**

- Check the number of selected tags.
- Verify that a fourth tag cannot be selected.
- Confirm that the published news contains the correct tags.
